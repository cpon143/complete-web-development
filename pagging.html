Select Your Glasses Type

Distance Glasses

Single vision glasses, these have one prescription throughout the whole lens and are used for seeing far away objects.

Reading Glasses

Single vision glasses used for near or intermediate vision. Your prescription will have a reading add to help you focus on close up objects.

Varifocal Glasses

Progressive glasses correct distance, intermediate and near vision in one lens. No need to switch between different glasses.

Frame only Glasses

Zero prescription can be used for style or with a blue light filter to protect your eyes from blue light whilst using digital screens.
